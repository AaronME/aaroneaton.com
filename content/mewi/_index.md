#### NOTE: this is a co-working session, not a critique group.

Join fun spirits ONLINE for writing and chatting, every Monday evening (Pacific Time). We encourage everyone's creativity and all are welcome! Our format is:

- 7:00PM - 15min chat
- 7:15PM - 45min quiet co-working
- 8:00PM - more chatting
- 8:15PM - more working
- 9:00PM - final chat
- 9:15PM - The End

We promote an atmosphere of completing your goals and hash out any questions that arise. We enjoy sparking creativity to finish your writing project, whatever it may beâ€”novel, short story, poem, memoir, comic, screenplay, etc.
